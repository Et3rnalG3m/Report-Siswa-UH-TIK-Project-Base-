Forged from Heritage, Engineered for Speed.

Garuda R adalah supercar flagship Nusantara yang memadukan aerodinamika modern, teknologi performa tinggi, dan filosofi desain yang terinspirasi dari warisan budaya Indonesia.

Design Philosophy

Keris Flow Design menggabungkan garis tajam seperti bilah keris dengan kurva organik terinspirasi ombak Nusantara.

  • Sharp keris-inspired lines
  • Dynamic aerodynamic curves
  • Wide stance for high stability
Exterior Highlights
  • Matrix LED Adaptive Headlights
  • Keris Slash Line
  • Garuda Wing Wheels
  • Active Rear Wing (Air Brake)
Interior Experience
  • 15” Digital Cluster & 13” Infotainment
  • Head-Up Display
  • Nappa leather + batik emboss
  • Teak wood veneer
  • Carbon fiber bucket seats (9 kg)
Performance
